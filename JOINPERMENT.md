# Join as a Permanent Member

Byair Browser is an open‑source project written in **C** with GTK3 and WebKit2GTK.  
We welcome contributors, volunteers, and community members who want to be part of the journey.  
This document explains how to become a **permanent member** and an **official volunteer**.

---

## 🛠 Steps to Join

1. **Start Contributing**  
   - Submit bug reports, feature requests, or documentation improvements.  
   - Open pull requests with clean code and clear commit messages.  
   - Participate in discussions about roadmap and design.

2. **Show Consistency**  
   - Active contributors who consistently help improve the project will be considered for permanent membership.  
   - Contributions can be code, documentation, testing, or community support.

3. **Apply for Membership**  
   - Once you have contributed regularly, you can apply to become a permanent member.  
   - Permanent members are recognized as official contributors and may receive certificates for volunteer work.

4. **Contact the Project Leader**  
   - To apply or ask questions, contact the project leader (CEO) directly:  
     **neo4linux404@proton.me**

---

## 🎓 Certificates for Volunteers
- Volunteers who contribute meaningfully will be eligible for an **official certificate of contribution**.  
- Certificates recognize your role in helping build Byair Browser during its development stage.  
- This can be used for your portfolio, CV, or professional recognition.

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
- Byair Browser is in **active development stage**.  
- Expect bugs, missing features, and rapid changes.  
- If you submit or include **malicious code, exploits, or harmful contributions**, the maintainers are **not responsible** for any resulting damage. Such contributions will be rejected.

Joining Byair Browser as a permanent member means being part of a hacker‑driven, open‑source project.  
We value curiosity, persistence, and innovation. Together, we can make everything possible.
